New York City

This is a collection of arts in New York city that I have been collecting since August 2023. It is true that the artworks at the museum are famous, but when I walk on the streets of New York, I can see art that is created by accident or by people freely. New York is a place where you can naturally experience art that represents the city itself even if you don't have to look for it. All collected by Young Kim.
